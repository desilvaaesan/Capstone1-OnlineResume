My Hobbies: More Than Just Code!

Hey there! When I’m not coding or working on cool projects, you’ll probably find me doing one of these:

🏸 Badminton

A sport that keeps me on my toes (literally). Whether it’s smashing, diving, or just trying not to miss the shuttle, badminton is my go-to way to stay active. I play on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays, and yes, I take it seriously, but still have fun!

🏃 Running

I started running to improve my stamina and endurance, and now it’s a regular part of my routine. I’m currently pushing myself for longer distances, and I even signed up for a 12km marathon! My goal? Finish strong and not collapse halfway through. 😂

🏀 Basketball

Nothing beats a good game of basketball with friends. It’s not just about shooting hoops—it’s about strategy, teamwork, and that one clutch three-pointer that makes you feel like an NBA star for five seconds.

🖥️ Tech & Coding

Okay, I know this is obvious, but I actually enjoy learning new technologies, solving problems, and building things. Whether it’s front-end, back-end, or something in between, I love diving into new frameworks and becoming a better developer every day.

🎮 Gaming

From competitive shooters to MOBA, gaming is my way to unwind. It’s also lowkey problem-solving, but with better graphics and less debugging (sometimes).

🍔 Trying New Food

I have a love-hate relationship with food. I want to eat healthy, but burgers, street food, and sweets keep calling my name. I enjoy exploring different flavors, especially when it involves a late-night snack run.

What About You?

If you relate to any of these (or have suggestions for a new hobby I should try), let’s talk! Always up for learning, competing, or just having a good time. 😆
